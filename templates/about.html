About Mind Care

Mind Care is a privacy-focused mental health support platform designed to provide immediate, supportive tools for anyone experiencing emotional distress, anxiety, or stress.

Our Mission

We believe that mental health tools should be accessible to everyone, without barriers. Our goal is to provide a calm, safe digital environment where users can practice mindfulness, journal their thoughts, and find helpful resources.

Privacy First

Mind Care runs locally on your machine. Your journal entries and chat history are stored in a local database and session, meaning your data stays with you.

Important Note

Mind Care is an AI-powered support tool and is **not** a replacement for professional therapy or medical intervention. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact professional services immediately.
