Guided Breathing (4-7-8)

Follow the expanding and contracting circle to guide your breath.
Inhale (4s), Hold (7s), Exhale (8s).

How it works

The 4-7-8 breathing technique, also known as "Relaxing Breath," has its roots in Pranayama yoga. It helps you gain control over your breath and can move your nervous system from "fight or flight" to "rest and digest."
