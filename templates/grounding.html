5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

This grounding exercise helps you focus on the present by engaging your five senses. It's particularly helpful for anxiety and panic.

5
THINGS YOU CAN SEE

Look around you and name five objects. A chair, a tree, a speck of dust, anything.

4
THINGS YOU CAN TOUCH

Acknowledge four things you can feel. The fabric of your clothes, the surface of your desk, or the wind on your face.

3
THINGS YOU CAN HEAR

Listen for three distinct sounds. The hum of a computer, birds chirping outside, or your own breath.

2
THINGS YOU CAN SMELL

Notice two smells. It could be the scent of your coffee, or just the smell of the air in the room.

1
THING YOU CAN TASTE

Focus on one thing you can taste. This could be a lingering taste from a meal or even just the inside of your mouth.
