Other Quick Tips

  • Physical Movement: Even a 5-minute walk can change your perspective.
  • Hydration: Sometimes a glass of cold water can help interrupt a spiral.
  • Naming Emotions: Saying "I am feeling [emotion]" can help reduce its power.
  • Music: Create a playlist of songs that make you feel safe or calm.
